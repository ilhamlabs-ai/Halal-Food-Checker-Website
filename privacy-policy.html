To capture clear photos of ingredient labels for instant analysis.

Storage Access

Internet Access

To send images securely to our AI service for real-time ingredient analysis.

Image Processing

Images you submit are processed in real-time through secure Firebase Cloud Functions using Google's infrastructure.
