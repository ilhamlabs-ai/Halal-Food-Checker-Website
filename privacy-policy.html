Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

Introduction

Halal Food Checker (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data and permissions used by the app.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.

Permissions Used

  • Camera: To capture ingredient label photos.
  • Storage: To select images from your gallery.
  • Internet: To send images securely to our AI service for analysis.

Image Processing

Images are processed in real-time through Firebase Cloud Functions. No images are stored or retained after analysis. All data is transmitted securely via HTTPS.

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Firebase (Google) – for secure cloud processing
  • Google Gemini AI – for ingredient recognition

All third-party services comply with GDPR and other privacy standards.

Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
